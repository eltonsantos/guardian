How does DNS blocking work?

When you configure a secure DNS on your router, every device on your network (phones, tablets, computers, smart TVs) is protected. Blocked websites simply don't load — regardless of browser, incognito mode, or operating system.

Why change the router password?

After configuring the DNS, changing your router's admin password and giving it to someone you trust makes the protection permanent. No one can undo the DNS settings without that password.

Privacy & transparency

Guardian Pro does not spy on traffic, does not monitor personal content, and does not send browsing history. Everything works through DNS rules + local extension rules, with a focus on ethics and privacy.
